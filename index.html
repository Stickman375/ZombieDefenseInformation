Zombie Defense Information

Controls: W=move up A=move left S=move down D=move right space=Shoot I=open inventory and x = close inventory e= eat food (if touching food ex:apple,steak)
Game Info: You have 5 hearts and 5 hunger keep your hunger up by eating and your health up by using medkits or staying warm in the fire if you have 5 sticks click the campfire to make the fire go up
More info: Try to avoid zombies as zombies as zombies have different skills but it wouldnt be fun to tell you the abilities :)
Good Luck and have fun survivors
